Swappable batteries to power your home and equipment
Hyperion is creating battery packs that can be used to backup your home or building and also power your electric equipment such as golf carts, lawn equipment, bikes, scooters, and much more. The packs will be swappable between all supported equipment and charge in a wall-mounted rack in your home or business. You can now power your mobile equipment and their homes/buildings with the same battery packs. Battery swapping eliminates charging downtime and uses DC solar power more efficiently.

You can save money on your electric bills by taking advantage of grid incentives, gain resilience against grid outages, and achieve energy independence. You can also buy fewer batteries overall since you won't need to buy discrete batteries for your home and all your equipment. It will be the most flexible and modular BESS available.
  • Power your house, transportation, and tools with the same batteries
  • Gain independence from the grid and never worry about outages again
  • Save money on your electricity bill
  • Swap packs in seconds
  • Buy fewer batteries
  • Use your solar power more efficiently
